Apple is one of the world’s most influential and innovative technology companies. Known for its premium products, elegant design, and strong ecosystem, Apple has transformed the way people use technology in their daily lives.

History and Foundation

Apple was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in California, USA. The company began with the Apple I computer and gained major recognition with the launch of the Apple II, one of the first successful personal computers. Over the years, Apple evolved from a computer manufacturer into a global technology leader.

Major Products

Apple is best known for its iconic products such as:

iPhone – a revolutionary smartphone that redefined mobile communication

MacBook & iMac – powerful and reliable personal computers

iPad – a versatile tablet for work, education, and entertainment

Apple Watch – a leading smartwatch focused on health and fitness
